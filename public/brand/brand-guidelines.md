# Precision Cut Concrete Removal LLC - Brand Guidelines

## Logo Usage
- Primary logo: `logo-primary.svg` for hero and large placements.
- Horizontal logo: `logo-horizontal.svg` for headers and footers.
- Mark only: `logo-mark.svg` for social icons, favicons, and compact spaces.
- Keep clear space around the logo equal to the height of the inner "P" bowl.
- Preferred background: Asphalt (#0B0F14) or Slate (#111827).

## Colors
- Concrete White: `#F8FAFC`
- Concrete Gray: `#E5E7EB`
- Steel: `#0F172A`
- Asphalt (secondary): `#111827`
- Safety Orange: `#F97316`
- Ember Orange: `#EA580C`

## Typography
- Headings: Oswald (bold, uppercase, wide tracking)
- Body: Inter (regular to semibold)

## Layout & Spacing
- Use wide tracking and uppercase headings.
- Use thick dividers and strong CTA buttons.
- Maintain a gritty, industrial feel with subtle concrete texture overlays.

## Iconography
- Use the custom SVG set in `/src/components/svg` for badges and UI icons.
- Keep strokes bold and consistent with the heavy, industrial style.
